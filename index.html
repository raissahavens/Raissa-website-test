Qualimentos Jr.

Cargill

"On Summer 2016 I did my food science internship at Cargill, in Minneapolis. I got to work in a bakery lab, where I developed my own project for sugar reduction on breads. I did a lot of different tests and made people try the breads to see what they thought about it, we call that sensory analysis. Probably, one of the best parts of the product development."
